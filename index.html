This is Me

I’m Jayson! A Multi-Disciplinary Creative in both Design and Arts. My creativity in Design involves works in Editorial, UX/UI, and branding. My creativity is also shown through my artistic hobbies in Dance and Photography. Born in Toronto raised in Brampton. I recently graduated from Humber Polytechnic with an Advanced Diploma in Graphic Design.

I'm all about growth and development. I take on any challenge as I know it helps me learn and improve. I'm open minded with projects to meet my client's wants and needs. I'm quick to adapt to any situations that arise. I do the best I can to be excellent even if it's a slow progress or I struggle. I make sure my projects make sense and there's purpose behind the design.
