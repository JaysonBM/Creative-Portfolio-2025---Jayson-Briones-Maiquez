True Hip-Hop Magazine

Editorial Design, Print Design, Art Direction, Layout, Typography, Imagery, Colour Theory

Creating my own zine. This was my second magazine project I worked on for the entire Editorial class for one semester in school. I created a magazine that reflects my own character involving the art of Hip-hop. I made sure to pay close attention to the fine detail of layout, typography, imagery, colours, and graphic elements. I wanted to make the readers feel interested and engaged in the magazine whether you're into hip-hop or at least interested in it. Maintaining the culture of hip-hop's cool aesthetics and playing around with their art styles.
