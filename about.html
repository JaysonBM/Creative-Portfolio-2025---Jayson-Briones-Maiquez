About

I'm Jayson Briones-Maiquez. A Filipino born in Toronto and raised in Brampton, Canada.

I recently graduated from Humber Polytechnic with an Advanced Diploma in Graphic Design. I'm focusing on developing my UX/UI design skills and building my portfolio.

I have a strong foundation in design principles and a passion for providing the best user experience. Always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow as a designer.

I'm looking for any Graphic Design role, including Junior Graphic Designer, UX/UI Designer, and Editorial Designer. I'm also open to freelance opportunities and internships as well. I want to continue developing my design skills and gain real-world experience in the industry.

I am excited to share my work with you and hope you enjoy exploring my portfolio. If you have any questions or would like to discuss a project, please feel free to reach out to me. I look forward to hearing from you!
